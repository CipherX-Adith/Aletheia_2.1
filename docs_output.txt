Project Philosophy
Aletheia is not merely an invoice tokenization platform. It is a trust infrastructure for global trade finance. Blockchain is the enabling infrastructure, while trust, transparency, liquidity, and automation are the core products.
Core Mission

ΓÇó Unlock liquidity for exporters without waiting for long payment terms.

Platform Ecosystem
Four Product Pillars
Trade Passport
Digital identity, compliance, reputation, trade history and Stellar wallet.
Trade Exchange
Marketplace where verified trade assets are financed.
Settlement Network
USDC settlement, Stellar assets, Soroban smart contracts and automated distributions.
Intelligence Layer
Risk engine, AI copilot, fraud detection and portfolio analytics.
Trade Asset
A Trade Asset is the investment unit. It combines:
- Commercial Invoice
- Purchase Order
- Bill of Lading
- Shipping Bill
- Insurance Information
- Exporter Trade Passport
- Buyer Profile
- Risk Score
- Verification Status
- Tokenization Metadata
